My Work

In my free time, I enjoy writing about AI and machine learning. I find it fascinating how these technologies are constantly evolving and changing the way we live and work. Writing about them allows me to deepen my understanding of these fields and stay up-to-date with the latest advancements.

I write on Medium, as it provides a platform for me to share my ideas and thoughts with a wider audience. I particularly focus on NLP within machine learning, as it has the potential to greatly impact the future. NLP can currently be used for tasks such as sentiment analysis, text classification, and language translation, but its potential applications are virtually limitless. I hope to use NLP successfully to implement it into an app and make a drastic change in a fixable problem. By combining my passion for writing and my interest in AI and machine learning, I hope to contribute to the ongoing discussions surrounding these technologies and their potential impact on society.
